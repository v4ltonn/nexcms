How this tool works

Your Sigma rule is sent securely to the NexCMS back-end, where a dedicated converter process (based on PySigma backends) generates equivalent queries for Microsoft Defender (KQL) or Elastic (Lucene).

  • Input size and processing time are strictly limited.
  • No rules are stored or logged beyond basic error metrics.
  • Designed not to impact the main news platform performance.
